Bechdel Impact in the Movie Universe

This visualization display bechdel data of the 100 highest rated movies between 1970 and 2013 on IMDb according to voter rating. The movies are classified and visualized according to their primary genre.

The Bechdel Test

The Bechdel test is a measure of the representation of women in fiction. It asks whether a work features at least two named women who talk to each other about something other than a man.
